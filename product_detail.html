mLLDPE EXCEED XP 8656ML

popis

An eXtreme Performance ethylene 1-hexene copolymer that offers step-out toughness, high flex-crack resistance and increased output with excellent bubble stability for a range of blown film applications. TnPP is not intentionally added to Exceed™ XP 8656ML. It contains processing aid and thermal stabilizer.

PRODUCT GROUP:

mLLDPE

Producer:

ExxonMobil Chemical

Density:

Agricultural Film, Blown Silage, Construction Liners, Flexible Packaging, Liquid Packaging,
